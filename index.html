About Us

Welcome to the Municipal Corporation of Indore Asset Management Portal
At the Municipal Corporation of Indore, we are dedicated to enhancing the efficiency and transparency of our city’s asset management. Our mission is to streamline asset tracking, predictive maintenance, and resource allocation to ensure that every corner of Indore is well-managed and optimized for the benefit of our citizens.

Our Vision

To create a smart, efficient, and responsive municipal system where every asset is accounted for, maintained, and utilized to its fullest potential, driving progress and improving the quality of life for all residents of Indore.
